 # Analysis of the Impact of Robotics on Employment

## Overview
This project presents a research-based analysis of how robotics and automation affect employment across industries.

It explores job displacement, creation of new roles, sector-specific impacts, and the ethical and socio-economic challenges of automation.

## Key Topics
- Automation and job displacement
- Creation of new roles in technology-driven sectors
- Sectoral impact across industries
- Ethical and economic implications
- Future workforce adaptation and reskilling

- Research paper in DOCX format
- Research paper in PDF format
